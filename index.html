About Our Story

New Era in Mourning & Death-care

Metamory is the future of commemoration, blending blockchain, XR, and AI to elevate the value of mourning. Our platform empowers individuals to create a personalized digital memorial for their loved ones in the Metaverse, preserving memories and honoring legacies.

At the heart of Metamory lies the Biography Exhibition, allowing users to construct a virtual biography house that showcases the deceased's life story through photos, videos, sounds, and 3D avatars. An AI 3D avatar acts as a curator, bringing the deceased to life in a new dimension and allowing them to remain present for the living.

Our unique Blockchain Graveyard uses NFT digital signatures to create an eternal and one-of-a-kind digital grave, offering a new level of commemoration. The Mourning System & Marketplace offers NFT offerings and grave designs, accessible on-platform and through secondary markets, all powered by our self-developed smart contract.

Metamory's Blockchain Family Tree takes things one step further, building an eternal and irreversible family tree that documents not just names but life stories and links to the biography house.

Join us on our mission to create a lasting legacy for the bereaved. With a blend of tradition, technology, and community, Metamory is the comprehensive solution for honoring and remembering loved ones.

Privacy Policy

Metamory, a social platform for pet owners, is committed to protecting the privacy of its users. This Privacy Policy describes how Metamory collects, uses, and shares personal information from users and outlines their rights under the GDPR.

Collection of Personal Information

Metamory collects personal information from users when they create an account, participate in discussions, or use other features of the platform. This information may include, but is not limited to, name, email address, profile picture, and location. Additionally, Metamory may collect information about users' pets, such as breed, age, and gender.

Use of Personal Information

Metamory uses personal information to provide users with a personalized experience and to improve the platform. This includes using information to suggest new friends, send notifications, and display personalized content. Metamory may also use personal information to comply with legal requirements, to enforce its policies, and to protect its rights and interests.

Sharing of Personal Information

Metamory does not sell, rent, or otherwise share personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes. Metamory may share personal information with service providers who assist with operating the platform, such as hosting providers, customer support, and analytics providers. These service providers are bound by contract to use personal information only for the purposes of providing their services to Metamory.

Data Retention

Metamory will retain personal information for as long as necessary to provide users with the services and features of the platform. Personal information may be deleted upon request, or when it is no longer needed for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy.

Rights of Users

Under the GDPR, users have the right to access their personal information and to request correction, deletion, or restriction of processing. Users also have the right to object to the processing of their personal information and to request that their personal information be transferred to another controller. To exercise these rights, users may contact Metamory at support@metamory.io.

Additional information regarding children's privacy

When the person who registers indicates that he or she is under 13 years old, we will either (i) collect no or limited personally identifiable information (e.g. e-mail address only) from that individual, (ii) inform the child that a parent's verifiable consent is required, or (iii) collect the email address of the user's parent in addition to the user's email address. We use the parent's email address to seek the parent's verifiable consent or notify the parent of his/her child's online activities and enable the parent to unsubscribe his/her child from a newsletter or other similar activity. If a user is under 13, we will not condition his/her participation in an online activity on the disclosure of more information than is reasonably necessary to participate in the activity. We do not share children's information with outside third parties not bound by this Privacy Policy for their own marketing purposes. If you would like to review any personally identifiable information that we have collected online from your child, have this information deleted from our active servers, and/or request that there be no further collection or use of your child's information or if you have questions about these information practices, you may contact us at support@metamory.io.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

Metamory may update this Privacy Policy from time to time and will post any changes on the platform. Users are encouraged to review this Privacy Policy regularly to stay informed about how Metamory is protecting their personal information.

Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or the way Metamory handles personal information, please contact us at support@metamory.io.
